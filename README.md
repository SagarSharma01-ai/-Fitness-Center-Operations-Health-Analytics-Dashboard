## 📌 Project Objective
This project replaces manual business tracking with a fully automated, interactive Power BI dashboard designed for gym and fitness center management. It bridges the gap between financial performance tracking and individual member health analytics.

## 💼 Business Requirements Addressed
1. **Overall Business Performance:** Automated the aggregation of total clients, trainers, revenue, expenses, and overall profit.
2. **Monthly Trends:** Side-by-side comparison of monthly member acquisition, revenue streams, and operational costs.
3. **Membership Management:** Granular tracking of membership tiers (Platinum, Gold, Silver) and real-time status updates (Active, Expired, Cancelled).
4. **Health & Fitness Calculator:** A dynamic DAX-powered engine that calculates physical metrics based on user input.
5. **Detailed Member Profiles:** Comprehensive tracking of member demographics, goals, and membership timelines.

## 🛠️ Key Technical Implementations
* **Interactive Data Modeling:** Connected multiple fact and dimension tables (Users, Payments, Expenses, ColorCodes) to create a seamless data flow.
* **Advanced DAX Formulas:** 
  * Created custom logic for the Calorie Calculator to instantly compute BMI, Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) based on varying activity levels.
* **High-End UI/UX Design:** 
  * Implemented custom theme switchers (e.g., "Blue Theme").
  * Designed an app-like navigation experience using Bookmarks and Selection Panes.
  * Utilized gauge charts, customized bar charts, and interactive tables for intuitive data consumption.
